Smart QR Attendance System

AI-powered attendance management using QR codes

The Smart QR Attendance System is a cross-platform mobile solution built using Flutter and Django that simplifies attendance tracking in schools and universities. Students scan a unique QR code while the system verifies their GPS location to ensure presence accuracy.
